After a couple of hours of struggling we realized that those 2 jars are missing (no matter if EMF was or not installed already):

org.eclipse.update.core_3.2.600.v20120530-1204.jar
org.eclipse.update.ui_3.2.300.v20100512.jar

And only that one was present

org.eclipse.update.configurator_3.3.200.v20120523-1752.jar

After a restart of the IDE... ZK studio installation was smooth as always. That workaround worked fine both under Windows7 and OSX.
